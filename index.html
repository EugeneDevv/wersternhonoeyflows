Bee Plants supply

Nectar and foliage are crucial in setting up a bee keeping enterprise. The amount of nectar determines the quantity and quality of honey you get. This is because bees take in nectar then digest is with enzymes then vomit it back as honey. This is why some people refer to honey as bee vomit. Thus before you set up an apiary it is crucial that you set up bee plants. We supply seedlings that are perennial and can survive in poor soils and climates. These plants are hardy but good for bees.
