Western Honey Flows lies in the front line to ensuring bee keepers are assisted in starting up and propping their bee keeping enterprises to commercial level for sustainable income. Foundation wax sheet are often used by bee hive manufacturers and placed inside the frames so as to ensure bees build combs properly. Bee Wax Candles

Bee wax candles are know to be purifiers. They emit negatively charge ions that bind to positively charged ion particles of dust, virus and bacteria floating in air thus neutralizing them. This way they eliminate contaminate air. Leading to purified air. Unlike petroleum candles bee wax candles do not drip and have a longer burn period.

Ksh 300
