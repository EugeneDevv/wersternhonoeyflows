Werstern Honey Flows is a leading social enterprise seeking to help clients eradicate poverty through establishment of sustainable bee keeping enterprise. The bee despite being important in balancing the ecosystem it is also a wealth creator. This wealth creation aspect needs research, elucidation, training and good management.

Western Honey Flows lies in the front line to ensuring bee keepers are assisted in starting up and propping their bee keeping enterprises to commercial level for sustainable income. Bee products such as propolis, bee venom, royal jelly, honey and bee pollen fetch a good price in the market and thus can offer alternative income. Start your journey today and begin to realise the bee is beneficial.
